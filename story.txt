Once upon a time, there was a college student. The student had many interests but the classes they took were not of interest to them. The student takes a computer science class during their second semester and loves it. He loved it so much that he studied 24 hours a day for the class.
Gregory Sorrell: He studied so intensely that the veins popped from his forehead and steam came out of his ears. His intensity could never be matched. Soon enough, his knowledge began to surpass that of his peers and he was dreaming in machine code.
